Services

About Us

Founders Rex and Betty Hendrix had a vision that became reality through the creation of the Spanish Oaks Ranch wedding and event center in 2006. The idea of land and beauty with others lives on as we continue to welcome our guests with determination to make each celebration here unforgettable.

We are family owned and operated, which makes for a friendly and personal environment. Our job is to help you enjoy your experience at Spanish Oaks Ranch and make your special event everything you hoped it would be. Let us remember the details. All you need to remember is your perfect day.
